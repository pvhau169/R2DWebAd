Short Description

R2D is an Online Food Ordering App that process food delivery or takeout from a local restaurant.

Processes

The app is powered by the following technologies


Android Studio/ Java

Firebase

App features

R2D app is designed to provide online order food and deliverty service. The food is provided from Golden Crown restaurant located in 7350 McArdle Rd . A customer will look at the menu, pick what they want, fill out their address, make a payment. Then, the food will be deliveried to them. There are three main features:

Personal account

Each customer have their own personal account to login to the app. The customer can view the menu, their order, and their bill to make payment.
The customer is provided two options: Sign In and Sign Up
For Sign Up options, the app requires the customer's phone, name and password to create a new account. If the new account is registed successfully, the customer can use it to Sign In in the next option.

Menu of restaurant

The app would show the detailed menu of the restaurant in the recycler view which includes: food name, example picture food price.
The customer will chose the food they want to order, choose how many disks they want, add them into the cart and make a payment.

Payments

For the online purchase, the customer is allowed make payment by using debit/credit card. They just need to fill their card number, the extended year and valid day.
In case the customer does not want to fill the infomation themselves. R2D provide the Scan option that can scan their card and fill the information (detailed information below)

Special features

Scanning Debit/Credit Cards

Instead of filling the card information yourself. The app provide the feature that can scan/read your cards and automaticaly fill in informations (card number, valid day, expended year)

Prices and distribution

This app is free to download use for all user located in US . All the revenue will come from selling food

Languages

Language used for this app is mainly English . Other languages like Spanish, French will be updated in the next version.
